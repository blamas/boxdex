## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

18" tapped horn with a folded path. The driver fires into a tapered horn that doubles
back on itself, cutting cabinet depth while keeping mouth area.

### Key design choices

- Horn mouth area: ~2400 cm²
- Horn expansion ratio: ~1.4
- Throat area matched to driver Sd
- Internal bracing every 300 mm

### Tuning

The design is optimised for flat response from 35 Hz upward. Low-pass at 80-100 Hz
when used in a system.
