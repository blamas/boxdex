## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

This 18" tapped horn design uses a folded path to achieve very high efficiency in a
compact footprint for the output level. The driver fires into a tapered horn that
doubles back on itself, reducing the overall cabinet depth.

### Key design choices

- Horn mouth area: ~2400 cm²
- Horn expansion ratio: ~1.4
- Throat area matched to driver Sd
- Internal bracing every 300 mm

### Tuning

The design is optimised for flat response from 35 Hz upward. Low-pass filtering at
80–100 Hz is recommended when used as a sub in a system.
