## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Two 10PLB76-8 woofers in a shared ported chamber, wired in parallel to a 4 Ω load. The
DE250 1" compression driver is mounted on a 90° waveguide. Active bi-amp required.

The single-woofer sim (`woofer.csv`) models one driver in the full cabinet volume, useful
for evaluating substitute 10" drivers against the 10PLB76 in the same alignment. The
combined system sim (`spl.csv`) shows the 3 dB gain from the second woofer and the full
HF extension through the DE250.

### Crossover

- LF/HF: 1800 Hz LR4 (24 dB/oct); DE250 minimum crossover is 1500 Hz
- High-pass at 65 Hz to protect woofers near excursion limit

### Wiring

Both woofers in parallel: 8 Ω + 8 Ω → 4 Ω nominal. Verify amp stability at 4 Ω.
DE250 on a separate 8 Ω channel.
