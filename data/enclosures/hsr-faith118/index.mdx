Single 18" front-loaded bass reflex designed by HSR sonorisation. Externally 600×704×800 mm
(338 L gross), 18 mm Finnish birch plywood. Designed around the RCF LF18X451.

Recommended subsonic HP filter: 29 Hz @ 24 dB/oct. Recommended system crossover: 90 Hz @ 24 dB/oct.

Sensitivity figures per the datasheet: 98 dB/W/m (1U), 103 dB/W/m (4U stacked).
Max SPL: 130 dB RMS / 141 dB peak at 4U with 6000 W.
