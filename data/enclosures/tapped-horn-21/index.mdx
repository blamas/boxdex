## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Large-format 21" tapped horn achieving sub-30 Hz extension at very high output levels.
The folded horn path keeps depth manageable given the cabinet volume.

### Key design choices

- Horn mouth area: ~4000 cm²
- Horn throat area matched to driver Sd (1620 cm²)
- Horn expansion ratio: ~1.45
- 18 mm birch ply throughout; 21 mm for baffle
- Internal bracing every 280 mm

### Tuning

Optimised for flat response from 30 Hz upward. Low-pass at 80-100 Hz recommended.
This box is heavy. Budget for a dolly or two-person carry.
