## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Classic 18" dual-chamber bandpass tuned for kick frequencies. The front chamber acts
as a high-pass filter and the rear chamber as a low-pass filter, producing a natural
bandpass response centred around 60-120 Hz with steep skirts outside that range.

### Chamber tuning

- Rear chamber: ~60 L, tuned at 52 Hz
- Front chamber: ~30 L, tuned at 115 Hz
- Both chambers vented with slot ports

### Usage

Typically crossed at 80-120 Hz to subs below and mids above. The steep bandpass roll-off
means minimal low-frequency bleed and no high-mid content: all kick, nothing else.
