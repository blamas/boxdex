## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Two 18SW115-8 drivers in a shared vented chamber, wired in parallel to a nominal 4 Ω load.
Both drivers share the same tuned port; the combined net volume gives a similar alignment
to the single-driver variant with extended low-end output.

### Port

- Two round ports, 150 mm diameter each
- Port length: 460 mm
- Tuning: 34 Hz (Fb)

### Wiring

Drivers wired in parallel: 8 Ω + 8 Ω → 4 Ω nominal. Check amp stability at 4 Ω before use.
