## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Two 12NBX100-8 drivers in a shared bass reflex enclosure, wired in parallel to present
4 Ω. The dual-12 format gives a larger Sd than a single 15" with a more compact
footprint per driver.

### Port

- Two round ports, 100 mm diameter each
- Port length: 310 mm
- Tuning: 44 Hz (Fb)

### Wiring

Both drivers in parallel: nominal 4 Ω load. Ensure the amplifier is 4 Ω rated.
Drivers should be matched (same production batch if possible) to keep displacement
symmetrical.
