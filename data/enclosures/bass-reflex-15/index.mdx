## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Straightforward 15" bass reflex sub using the 15SW115-8. Smaller and lighter than the
18" equivalent, suitable when weight is a constraint. The SW series motor provides
higher Xmax than the DS variant, which helps at the lower Fb.

### Port

- Dual round ports, 100 mm diameter each
- Port length: 340 mm
- Tuning: 38 Hz (Fb)

### Usage

Low-pass at 80–100 Hz. Pairs well with the 15" mid horn for a compact two-way system.
