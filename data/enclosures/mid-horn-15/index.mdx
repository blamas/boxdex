## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

15" front-loaded horn covering the mid band from roughly 120 Hz to 3–4 kHz. The horn
loading provides a significant efficiency gain over a direct-radiator midrange at the
same power level, making this suitable for high-output sound systems.

### Horn geometry

- Mouth area: ~3600 cm²
- Throat area: matched to driver Sd
- Expansion ratio: ~1.3 per section
- Folded path inside the cabinet to keep depth manageable

### Usage

Cross to a sub or kickbin below (~80–120 Hz) and to a high-frequency horn or top above
(~3–4 kHz). High-pass at 100 Hz is strongly recommended to protect the driver.
