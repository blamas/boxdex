## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

A 2-way top pairing a 12" B&C 12NDL76 woofer with a B&C DE250 1" compression driver on
a 90°×60° waveguide (e.g. 18 Sound XT1086). Demonstrates a mixed driver table: cone
woofer and compression driver in one enclosure.

### Crossover

- Recommended crossover ≥ 1500 Hz to stay above the DE250's usable lower limit
- The CD mates any 1" exit horn; coverage is set by the chosen waveguide

### Usage

Flown or pole-mounted on a sub. High-pass at 80 Hz.
