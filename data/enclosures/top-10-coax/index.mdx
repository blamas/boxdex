## Build notes

**Sample data: values are illustrative, not measured.**

Compact 10" coaxial top using the 10CXN64 (coaxial neodymium, 6.4 cm² throat compression
driver). Measured in ground-plane conditions at 1 m with a calibrated microphone in REW.

### Port

- Single rear slot port, 60 mm × 160 mm
- Port depth: 140 mm
- Tuning: 80 Hz (Fb)

### Usage

Suitable as a small fill or near-field monitor. High-pass at 100 Hz recommended.
The coaxial driver provides good point-source behaviour for time-alignment.
