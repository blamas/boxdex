Compact 12" bass-reflex top enclosure. The measurement was taken in ground-plane
conditions at 1 m with a calibrated microphone in REW.

- Slot port at the rear, 80 mm × 200 mm
- Port depth: 180 mm
- Tuning: 65 Hz (Fb)

Typically flown or placed on a sub. High-pass filter at 80 Hz is recommended.
