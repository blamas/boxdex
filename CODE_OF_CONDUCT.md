# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Boxdex is a public commons for loudspeaker design. It exists so builders and
organisers can share knowledge without gatekeeping. That only works if the
community around it is safe to participate in, regardless of experience level,
background, gender, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race, ethnicity,
age, religion, or nationality.

We commit to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the wider community.
- Show empathy towards other contributors.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory remarks, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualised language or imagery, unwelcome attention, or advances.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its
community.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a private
security advisory on the repository (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the
link) or by contacting a maintainer listed in
[CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS) directly. Reports will be reviewed and
investigated, and a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the
circumstances will follow. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy
and security of the reporter.

Maintainers who violate this Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent
repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
